- ABOUT US -

TREVOR DA SILVA

I've been obsessed with games since forever. Spending many nights falling asleep with a controller in hands or my head hitting the keyboard. I never even considered that I could work in the games industry, or even create my own games, and yet here I am. In 2012 I took the Game Design program at Vancouver Film School. It was an amazing experience with tons of learning, and connections made that allowed me to get into the industry. My first job after VFS was Quality Assurance at EA. After 4 years of working with some incredible and talented people, I was struggling with working on games outside of work. I met Kyle at EA and we had been trying to work on games together for awhile but nothing was ever getting past protoype phases. So I ended up quitting and taking 1 year off so we could release our first game, Batter Up VR. Presently, I work full time as a Software Engineer at a game studio in Vancouver, and I also have our own company Polygon Dust, where we're working on releasing our 3rd game.

Explore the woods, survive the night. Arriving late to the abandoned Oakwood campground, you find the violent remains of your friends' campsite. - IN DEVELOPMENT -

Destructo Race is a local multiplayer (1-4 players) racing game with a twist.

DESTRUCTION

Some traps, like bombs and landmines have the capability for destruction. When a destruction trap is triggered, it can destroy parts of the track, destroy other traps, and even knock players around

TRAPS

Players place permanent traps onto the track as they race which leads to more and more chaos as the race continues. We currently have ramps, sticky honey, land mines, oil slicks, boxing glove machines, cannons, speed strips, and several others.

BALANCE

All of this comes at a price, however, as the more chaos and destruction you sow, the harder it will be for you to race as well. You must strike a balance when placing traps so you can finish the race while your opponents cannot.

MULTIPLE WAYS TO WIN

Not everyone is at the same skill level when it comes to racing, so winning doesn't always mean coming in first. Players are awarded style points at the end of the race for various actions performed while racing, such as number of bumps, number of times their trap blew someone off the map, number of traps placed, fastest lap, most decorations destroyed, and more.

EASY TO PICK UP, HARD TO MASTER

We believe anyone should be able to play, but we want to reward those that spend the effort to become skilled at the game. Our menus will be designed in a way to get you and up to three friends into a game very quickly, with the ability to quickly choose common rule sets/groups of traps. As you get more comfortable with the game, you will have the option to customize gameplay through various mode settings, trap selection, and handicap settings per player.
